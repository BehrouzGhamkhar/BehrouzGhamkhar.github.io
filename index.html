Some of my past projects

Drift Master

Drift Master is an action racing game where you have to overtake or blow up your opponents and cross the finish line. I mostly handled the VFX and SFX for this game as well as designing environments and implementing mechanics, features and in-game store.

Game Center IDLE

In the summer of 2022, my teammate Amir Hossein and I were learning about GOAP architecture. We decided to make a tycoon-like idle game with it. By using the GOAP architecture, there is no need to produce complicated FSMs to cover all possible situations, and the NPC can decide and act on its own. I also challenged my 3D modelling skills in this project, and I made most of the 3D models using Blender.

Bike Master

We Built this game at Peaky Pie Studio. I mostly handled VFX, SFX, visuals and impelemented game UI and designed environments.

Merge Master

Merge Master is a puzzle game that I made in a few days. My goal was to create an engaging puzzle with a beautiful environment. You control a cube and merge it with other cubes that have the same value.
